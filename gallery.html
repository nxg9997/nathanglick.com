No One Left Behind

NOLB is a 2.5D mini-adventure game which places you in a dark forest with the goal of rescuing all of the lost girls in the forest before finding the exit. Each rescued girl increases the brightness of your light, making it easier to see your surroundings. This game was made at a Game Jam at Ritsumeikan University in Japan, and won first place.

Website

E.C.H.O.

E.C.H.O. places you in the shoes of a human test subject who lost their vision during an experiment. Using the power of sound and echolocation, you must travel through a series of dark mazes.
